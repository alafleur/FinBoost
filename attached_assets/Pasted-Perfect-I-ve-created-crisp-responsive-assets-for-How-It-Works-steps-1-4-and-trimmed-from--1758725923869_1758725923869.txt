# Replit implementation (drop-in)

1. **Add the files**
   Place the 12 PNGs in your public assets folder (e.g., `/client/public/assets/howitworks/`), keeping the same filenames.

2. **Update the component** (`HowItWorks.tsx` or wherever Steps 1–4 are rendered)

Use `srcSet` + `sizes` so browsers auto-pick the right density:

```tsx
// example for Step 1
<img
  src="/assets/howitworks/howitworks-step1@1x.png"
  srcSet="/assets/howitworks/howitworks-step1@1x.png 1x,
          /assets/howitworks/howitworks-step1@2x.png 2x,
          /assets/howitworks/howitworks-step1@3x.png 3x"
  sizes="(max-width: 640px) 90vw, (max-width: 1024px) 45vw, 400px"
  alt="How it works – Step 1"
  className="rounded-2xl shadow-lg"
  loading="lazy"
/>
```

Repeat for steps 2–4, swapping the filename.

3. **Optional: central helper**
   If your How It Works cards are a map/array, a tiny helper keeps things tidy:

```tsx
const stepImg = (n: 1|2|3|4) => ({
  src: `/assets/howitworks/howitworks-step${n}@1x.png`,
  srcSet: `/assets/howitworks/howitworks-step${n}@1x.png 1x, /assets/howitworks/howitworks-step${n}@2x.png 2x, /assets/howitworks/howitworks-step${n}@3x.png 3x`,
});
```

4. **Styling nits**

* Keep images at a consistent **max-width \~400px** in the card to match your typography scale.
* Ensure container has `overflow-hidden` + `rounded-2xl` so edges look crisp on light backgrounds.

5. **QA checklist**

* Verify retina sharpness on MacBook/iPhone (no blur at 2x/3x).
* Confirm no layout shifts (CLS) — `width`/`height` attrs or a fixed container ratio can help if needed.
* Lighthouse: ensure `loading="lazy"` is applied where off-screen.
