### Why this should read “pro” (and not generic)

* **Massive type + whitespace**: the authority comes from restraint and scale, not decorations.
* **Left alignment** like Underdog; center-aligned heroes often feel “marketing site.”
* **No fake numbers, no demo assets, no phone frames**—everything authentic.
* **Static Tailwind classes only** (no runtime template strings), so JIT/purge is safe.
* **Simple accent** on the right (optional). Delete that `<div>` for a pure white hero.

---

## Tiny knobs to tune (if it still feels off to you)

These are the 3 levers that change the “feel” the most:

1. **Weight of the headline**

   * Current: `font-extrabold tracking-tight lg:tracking-tighter`
   * Try tighter letter-spacing: add `tracking-[-0.02em]` to the headline for a denser, “sportsbook” vibe.

2. **Space rhythm**

   * Section padding: `py-24 md:py-32 lg:py-36`. If it feels cramped, bump each by one step (`py-28/36/40`).
   * Gap before CTAs: `mt-8` → try `mt-9` or `mt-10` for more calm.

3. **Accent presence**

   * If the right-side wash feels like “extra,” remove it entirely (delete the absolute `<div>`).
   * Or make it bolder by changing opacities: `rgba(59,130,246,0.12)` / `rgba(147,51,234,0.10)`.
