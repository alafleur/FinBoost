You’ve got the message and tech in a good place. What’s missing is that “calm, premium” composition: bigger air, tighter typographic hierarchy, fewer flourishes. Here’s a focused plan + drop-in code you can hand to Replit to lock this down fast (with a future slot for your explainer video).

# What to change (design rules)

1. Layout

* Use a single, generous column on mobile. On desktop, a simple 12-col split: text (7) + optional media (5).
* Kill any badges/pills above the headline. The hero should start with the H1.
* Keep only two CTAs on one row.

2. Typography

* Two-line headline with clear hierarchy: line 1 uses a subtle blue→purple gradient; line 2 in solid near-black.
* Tight tracking, no shadow, no strokes.
* Subhead sits at a modest size; don’t oversell it.

3. Whitespace & rhythm

* Top/bottom padding: \~96px mobile, \~128px+ desktop.
* Max width for text block: 700–760px.
* Space between headline and subhead: 16–20px. Between subhead and CTAs: 24px.

4. CTAs

* Primary: filled gradient; Secondary: quiet outline.
* Button height \~48px (not jumbo), medium weight, rounded-lg.

5. Legal microcopy

* One quiet line below the CTAs (same as you have): “No purchase necessary…” Don’t add icons.

6. Media (for later)

* Replace the right-side phone with a clean **16:9 video poster** box. No phone frame, no tags. Just a soft shadow and a centered play icon. (Until the video is ready, the box stays as a poster image.)
