You’re right—no placeholders. Let’s keep it razor-clean and centered so the headline + CTAs do the work, and add only light, credible proof chips. No images, no frames, no scaling risk.

Below is a **drop-in hero** that:

* centers everything (desktop & mobile),
* uses only static Tailwind classes (safe with purge/JIT),
* has two clear CTAs,
* includes small trust chips (optional),
* keeps your legal line,
* and adds zero media/placeholder elements.

```tsx
// ...

```

---

## Why this will stick

* **No media at all** → nothing to scale or blur, nothing for Tailwind to purge.
* **One clear H1**, tight subhead, **two CTAs**, and a single legal line.
* **Trust chips** are plain text + an inline icon—easy to remove if you want it even more minimal.

---

## Optional polish (still zero media)

If you want a hair more “premium” without introducing images:

* Add a **very light background gradient** behind the section (kept to white-ish tones so it never competes):

```html
<section className="relative bg-gradient-to-b from-white via-white to-slate-50">
```

* Or a **thin underline** under the second headline line:

```html
<span className="mx-auto mt-3 block h-[2px] w-16 rounded bg-gradient-to-r from-blue-600 to-purple-600" />
```

(Place it right after the second `<span>` in the H1.)
